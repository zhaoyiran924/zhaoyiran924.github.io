Yiran Zhao

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate at National University of Singapore advised by Prof. Kenji Kawaguchi. I also closely work with Michael Shieh and Wenxuan Zhang. I completed my undergraduate at Shanghai Jiaotong University under Prof. Haiming Jin's advisor. My research is primarily centered around exploring Interpretability, Multilingual, Alignment, and Reasoning within the context of Large Language Models.
